NIST Cybersecurity Framework

  Use the CSF to find your blind spots and build a cybersecurity roadmap to protect what matters most.

We understand that cybersecurity shouldn't get in the way of your business - that's why we leverage the flexibility of the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) to build stronger cybersecurity programs and find practical solutions to address cyber risk.

Members of our team worked closely with NIST during the initial development of the CSF and continue to be involved in its evolution today. Our team has been conducting Cybersecurity Framework Assessments and helping companies across sectors implement the CSF since it was originally released in 2014.

The Cybersecurity Framework has become the industry standard over the last decade and is used across sectors to improve and measure cybersecurity capabilities. At Optic, we work with organizations to evaluate where they are today, identify their blind spots, and define a secure path forward.

  • Understand where you are today
  • Catalog your cybersecurity risks
  • Target where you need to be
  • Define a path forward
  • Protect what matters most

Whether you're just starting out or you already have a well-defined program, our cybersecurity engineers can help you meet your cybersecurity goals. Our team helps to ensure you are focused on the things that matter most and eliminate any blind spots in your cybersecurity program. As a third-party assessor, we can review your program without preconceived ideas or expectations to ensure you have the right cybersecurity capabilities to meet your business goals.

Reach out to work with our team of experts and make sure you're managing your cybersecurity risk!
