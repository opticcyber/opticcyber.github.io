CMMC

  From understanding your requirements, to getting ready for certification, Optic can help you through each step of your CMMC journey.


At Optic Cyber Solutions, we help companies build programs to securely handle Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) and protect our nation's supply chain.

The Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) isn't about finding the quickest way to “check the box” or who can create the tallest pile of documentation, it's about building security into the way we operate.

Optic is proud to be an authorized CMMC Registered Practitioner Organization (RPO). As an RPO, Optic has partnered with companies to prepare them for CMMC certification and is excited to help you meet your CMMC goals.


  • CMMC Readiness Assessment & Gap Review
  • Cybersecurity Policy & Plan Development
  • System Security Plan (SSP) Development with Plan of Action & Milestones (POA&M)
  • CMMC Consultation and Preparation Services
  • CUI Boundary Scoping Assistance

We understand the challenges CMMC brings to organizations and believe that cybersecurity shouldn't be a roadblock. We are committed to helping organizations address these challenges with a risk-based approach that turns CMMC requirements into business enablers.

Our engineers are experienced with implementing cybersecurity controls and have conducted hundreds of assessments across diverse sectors as well as supported the development of the Certified CMMC Professional (CCP) Field Guide & Exam Prep Manual. Through this experience, we are uniquely qualified to help your organization gain efficiencies and avoid common pitfalls when trying to address CMMC requirements and prepare for upcoming certifications.
